Ingredients:
4 eggs
cream cheese
1 can of condensed milk

Steps:
1. Heat the oven to 350F.
2. Separate the egg yolk from the white.
3. Beat the yokl with electric mixer until it is well-mixed and creamy.
4. Add the cream cheese to the yolk and mix with electric mixer.
5. Add the condensed milk to the cream cheese and egg yolk mixture until fully blended.
6. Add the whites to the mixture using spatula.
7. Using a hand beater, mix until fully blended.
8. Put the mixture into a container.
9. Bake in the oven for 50 minutes.
10. Enjoy!!!!
