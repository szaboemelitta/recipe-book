For the Dough:
1 package (2 ¼ teaspoons) active dry yeast
1 cup warm water
1 tablespoon granulated sugar
2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon olive oil
¾ teaspoon salt 

For the Sauce & Toppings:
¾ cup pizza sauce
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
¼ cup shredded Parmesan cheese
Desired toppings 

Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to a high temperature, ideally 475°F to 500°F (245°C to 260°C), for at least 15-20 minutes.
Shape Dough: Punch down the risen dough, shape it into a 12-inch circle on a floured surface, and transfer to a baking sheet or pizza pan.
Add Toppings: Brush the crust edge with olive oil, spread pizza sauce, top with cheeses, and add other desired toppings.
Bake: Bake for about 10 to 15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
Finish: Let cool briefly before slicing and serving.
